# Joining the Trillion Builders Org

This repo is public — anyone can read it, fork it, and learn from it.

But to contribute directly (submit PRs, push to the library), you need to be a member of the Trillion Builders GitHub org. Membership is kept intentionally small and manually approved.

---

## How to Request Access

We don't have a form. We have people.

Reach out to the OGs on **X (Twitter)** or **Telegram** and drop:

- Your **GitHub username** (so we can find your account)
- A quick note on **who you are** and what you're trying to build or contribute
- How you found us

We'll take a look, verify you're a real human, and get you in.

---

## Why We Do It This Way

Anyone can read this library. That's intentional — the knowledge is public.

But the people who *write* to it are vetted. Not because we're exclusive, but because we're protecting the quality of what gets built here. Every contributor has skin in the game.

---

*If you're the right fit, we'll know.*
